Home / Sponsors

FTC doesn't run on enthusiasm alone.

Robot parts, registration fees, travel, tools. Our sponsors make every season possible — and their logos travel with us to every competition.

Why Sponsor

What your support does.

A typical FTC season costs us roughly $8,000 — registration, robot parts, travel to qualifiers and championships, and outreach materials. Every dollar goes directly into the students' hands.

In return: visible recognition at every event we attend, on every shirt we print, in every interview we give to local press, and on this website. More importantly, you're investing in students who'll be the engineers, technicians, and entrepreneurs of tomorrow — many of whom will return to communities like ours.

We're a 501(c)(3).

Donations are tax-deductible. We provide acknowledgment letters and W-9s on request, and we'll happily share our annual budget and end-of-season report.
