Eleven seasons. One team.

Every year FIRST drops a new game in September. Every year we build a new robot to play it. Here's the full record — robots, results, notebooks, and what we learned.

The Archive

Every season, documented.

Team 9960 was founded in 2015 and has competed every FTC season since — from RES-Q as a rookie team to DECODE in 2025–26. Each season has its own page with the robot we built, how we did, the engineering notebook, and photos from the year.

Older seasons may have less detail than recent ones; that's just how memory works. We're filling in past seasons as students and mentors dig up old photos and notes.

02 / Looking Forward

The next season.

2026–27

FIRST reveals each season's game on a Saturday in early September. Until then, the next season is a mystery — to us as much as anyone.

Our 2026–27 season kicks off at the FIRST Tech Challenge reveal in September 2026. A new game, a fresh field, a blank-slate robot. We'll start prototyping that same week.

If you're thinking about joining the team for the upcoming year, late summer is a good time to reach out — we'll have a sense of who's returning and what spots are open.
