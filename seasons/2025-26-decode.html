2025–26 · DECODE.

An archaeology-themed shooting game. Color-coded artifacts, sequenced scoring, and an endgame climb. Our eleventh competitive season.

The Game

What DECODE was.

FIRST revealed DECODE on September 6, 2025. Robots scored by collecting color-coded artifacts from the field and launching them at scoring goals — bonus points came from completing color sequences. The endgame asked robots to climb onto a raised platform.
